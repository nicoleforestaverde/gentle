Privacy Policy

Gentle, soft reminders. Effective date: 5 July 2026.

The short version. Gentle does not collect your personal data. There is no account, no server, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking. Your reminders never leave your device.

2. Data we do not collect

Gentle runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to:

  • your reminders ("nudges"), lists, or their contents
  • your name, email address, or any account data (no account exists)
  • your location, contacts, photos, or files
  • usage analytics, crash reports, or advertising identifiers

3. Data stored on your device

Your reminders, completed items, theme, language, and app icon preferences are stored locally on your device only. They are never transmitted to us or to anyone else. Deleting the app permanently deletes this data. If you use the export feature, a copy is written only to the location you choose.

4. Notifications

Gentle uses local notifications scheduled on your device to remind you of your reminders. No push notification service is used, and no device token is sent anywhere. Notifications are optional. The app works fully if you decline the permission.

5. Purchases (Gentle Plus)

If you subscribe to Gentle Plus, the payment is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We never see your payment details.

To validate subscriptions, the app uses RevenueCat (RevenueCat, Inc.) as a processor. RevenueCat receives the store purchase receipt together with a randomly generated, anonymous app user ID. This ID is not linked to your identity, and we do not combine it with any other data. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract). See the RevenueCat privacy policy.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, and port personal data, to restrict or object to its processing, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Because Gentle does not collect personal data, in practice there is usually nothing for us to disclose or delete. Your data stays on your device and under your control. For any request, contact info@softailed.com.

7. Children

Gentle does not collect data from anyone, including children, and contains no ads or external content feeds.

8. Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Gentle handles data, we will update this page and the effective date above before the change takes effect.
