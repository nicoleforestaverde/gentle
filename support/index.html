Frequently asked questions

How do nudges work?

Add a nudge with the + button, pick roughly when it should come back (today, tomorrow, the weekend, someday) and which part of the day suits it, from morning to night, all day, or a specific time. Gentle picks a moment inside that window and sends a notification. Miss it and the nudge moves to the next part of the day and tries again, until you mark it done or move it.

Two kinds do not wander. An All day nudge sits in Notification Center and renews at midnight. A specific-time nudge arrives at its time and only comes back if you gave it a repeat.

I am not receiving notifications.

Most nudges are silent by default: Notification Center and the lock screen, badge, no sound. Specific-time nudges are the exception, and they can come through Focus. Any other nudge stays silent unless you switch Sound on for it.

Tap a notification and Gentle opens on that nudge, with its card already open.

If nothing arrives at all, check that notifications are allowed for Gentle in Settings, then Notifications, then Gentle. Gentle uses local notifications, so no connection is needed.

If they arrive but at odd times, check the online hours in Preferences. Gentle only lands part-of-day nudges inside that window, so a narrow one pushes them to its edge. Specific-time and All day nudges keep their own timing regardless.

What does Someday do?

Someday is a shelf with no day attached. A nudge left there speaks once, 14 days later, at its own part of the day. It never repeats and never wanders. Open its card any time to give it a real day, and re-shelving starts the 14 days over.

What is the free limit?

3 active nudges in total, not per day. Someday and anything shared in from another app count too; the two examples Gentle starts you with do not. Completing or deleting one frees a slot.

At the limit, sharing into Gentle stops as well: you get the paywall, and closing it without subscribing means the shared text is not kept. Gentle Plus removes the limit and unlocks all 19 palettes and repeating nudges.

How do I move my data to a new device?

In Preferences, under Your data, tap Back up. The file holds your nudges, your Done list and your settings.

Save it somewhere the new device can reach, such as Files or iCloud Drive, then open Preferences there and tap Restore. Gentle cannot be opened straight from a shared file, so it has to be saved first. You will be asked to confirm before a restore replaces anything.

How do I cancel my Gentle Plus subscription?

Preferences, then Manage subscription, which opens Apple's subscription screen. You can also get there through Settings, your name, Subscriptions. Deleting the app does not cancel it.

I reinstalled or changed device. Where is my subscription?

Open the paywall and tap Restore purchases. Gentle Plus is tied to your App Store account, not to the device or the app data, so signing in with the same account gets it back.

Where is my data stored? Can you recover it?

Your nudges live on your device, plus wherever you choose to keep a backup file. Gentle has no account and no server of ours, and your nudges are never sent anywhere. Each time you open Gentle it asks RevenueCat, the service that tracks subscriptions, whether Gentle Plus is active on your store account. That check carries an anonymous ID and your store receipt, and nothing else. If you subscribe, the payment itself goes through Apple. The Privacy Policy has the details.

Because we hold nothing, we cannot recover anything after the app is deleted or the device is reset, unless you saved a backup. A full device backup includes Gentle.

How do I delete all my data?

Delete the app. That removes everything Gentle stored on the device, though a backup file you saved earlier stays wherever you put it, and a Gentle Plus subscription still needs cancelling separately.

Which languages are available?

English, German, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Korean and Japanese. Gentle follows your device language when it is one of these, and you can also choose one in Preferences.
