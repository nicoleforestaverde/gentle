Frequently asked questions

How do nudges work?

Add a nudge with the + button, pick roughly when it should come back (today, tomorrow, the weekend, one day) and which part of the day feels right. Gentle schedules a soft notification in that window. Nothing expires and nothing turns red. A nudge simply waits until you get to it.

I am not receiving notifications.

Check that notifications are allowed for Gentle in your device settings (Settings, then Notifications, then Gentle on iOS; Settings, then Apps, then Gentle, then Notifications on Android). Also make sure Focus or Do Not Disturb is not silencing them. Gentle uses only local notifications, so no internet connection is needed for them to arrive.

What is the free limit?

The free version holds up to 3 nudges per calendar day. Gentle Plus unlocks unlimited nudges, all 26 themes, and repeating nudges.

How do I move my data to a new device?

Use the export option in Gentle to save a backup file of your reminders, then open that file with Gentle on your new device to import it. Your data moves with the file. It is never uploaded to a server.

How do I cancel my Gentle Plus subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by the store, not inside the app.
iOS: Settings, then your name, then Subscriptions, then Gentle Plus, then Cancel.
Android: Play Store, then profile, then Payments and subscriptions, then Subscriptions.
Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

I reinstalled or changed device. Where is my subscription?

Open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases. As long as you are signed in with the same Apple Account or Google account you bought it with, your Gentle Plus access is restored. The subscription is tied to your store account, not to the device or the app data.

Where is my data stored? Can you recover it?

Everything lives on your device only. Gentle has no server and no account. That also means we cannot recover reminders after the app is deleted or the device is reset, unless you saved an export file. If you back up your device, Gentle data is included in that backup.

How do I delete all my data?

Delete the app. Because nothing is stored anywhere else, that removes everything permanently.

Which languages and themes are available?

Gentle speaks English, German, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Korean, and Japanese, and ships 26 themes, each with light and dark variants, plus matching alternate app icons.
